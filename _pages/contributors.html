Project Leadership

Ashlyn Stewart, PhD, founding co-director and project editor, is a digital scholarship specialist for the Boston College Libraries' Digital Scholarship Group. Ashlyn serves as project manager, trains and oversees a team of research assistants to extract and structure the almanacs data, and creates visualizations with the data.

Seth Meehan, PhD, founding co-director, is the associate director of academic and programming and special projects for the Boston College Libraries. Seth works with stakeholders across and beyond the university to identify opportunities for data from the almanacs to advance reserach.
